Adali Montoya

Oil painter

Adali Montoya is a visual artist whose work explores form, color, and emotion through figurative imagery. Her practice centers on the human body as a site of tension and tenderness, using atmosphere, light, and composition to evoke intimacy, power, and vulnerability.

Her work is guided less by narrative and more by sensation, inviting viewers into moments that feel suspended in time. Through layered surfaces and quiet intensity, Montoya creates images that linger—resisting easy interpretation and encouraging prolonged, personal engagement.
