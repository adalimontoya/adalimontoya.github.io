Oil painter
Adali Montoya is a visual artist whose work explores form, color, and emotion through figurative imagery. Her practice centers on the human body as a site of tension and tenderness.

Through layered surfaces and quiet intensity, Montoya creates images that linger—inviting prolonged, personal engagement.
